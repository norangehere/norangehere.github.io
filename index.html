Publications

Brain4FMs: A Benchmark of Foundation Models for Electrical Brain Signal

Fanqi Shen, Enhong Yang, Jiahe Li, Junru Hong, Xiaoran Pan, Zhizhang Yuan, Meng Li, Yang Yang

arXiv2026

Introduces a comprehensive benchmark for Brain Foundation Models (BFMs) on electrical brain signals, systematically evaluating mainstream foundation models on neural data. This work advances the AI + Neuroscience intersection.

Arcadia: Toward a Full-Lifecycle Framework for Embodied Lifelong Learning

Minghe Gao, Juncheng Li, Yuze Lin, Xuqi Liu, Jiaming Ji, Xiaoran Pan, Zihan Xu, Xian Li, Mingjie Li, Wei Ji, Rong Wei, Rui Tang, Qizhou Wang, Kai Shen, Jun Xiao, Qi Wu, Siliang Tang, Yueting Zhuang

NeurIPS2025

CVPR2025

Proposes a geo-localization inference framework based on fine-tuned Vision-Language Models (VLMs), achieving multi-granular visual cue extraction and reasoning through enhanced Reasoning Chains.
