Publications

Brain4FMs: A Benchmark of Foundation Models for Electrical Brain Signal

Fanqi Shen, Enhong Yang, Jiahe Li, Junru Hong, Xiaoran Pan, Zhizhang Yuan, Meng Li, Yang Yang

arXiv2026

Presents an open benchmark and evaluation platform for Brain Foundation Models on electrical brain signals, organizing the field through a self-supervised learning taxonomy and a task-and-dataset view. The work integrates 15 representative BFMs with 18 public datasets to analyze how pretraining data, SSL strategy, and model architecture influence downstream generalization and transfer.

Arcadia: Toward a Full-Lifecycle Framework for Embodied Lifelong Learning

Minghe Gao, Juncheng Li, Yuze Lin, Xuqi Liu, Jiaming Ji, Xiaoran Pan, Zihan Xu, Xian Li, Mingjie Li, Wei Ji, Rong Wei, Rui Tang, Qizhou Wang, Kai Shen, Jun Xiao, Qi Wu, Siliang Tang, Yueting Zhuang

NeurIPS2025

Proposes a closed-loop full-lifecycle framework for embodied lifelong learning, arguing that real progress requires tightly coupling data acquisition, scene reconstruction and augmentation, shared multimodal representation learning, and sim-from-real evaluation. Arcadia shows that this end-to-end improvement loop yields consistent gains on navigation and manipulation benchmarks while transferring robustly to physical robots.

GRE Suite: Geo-localization Inference via Fine-Tuned Vision-Language Models and Enhanced Reasoning Chains

Chun Wang, Xiaojun Ye, Xiaoran Pan, Zihao Wang, Haofan Wang, Yiren Song

CVPR2025

Introduces a reasoning-augmented geo-localization framework built around three components: the GRE30K reasoning dataset, a multi-stage GRE model for progressively inferring scene attributes and local details, and GREval-Bench for evaluating localization from continent and country level down to city and street level. The method improves both accuracy and interpretability over prior VLM-based geo-localization approaches.
